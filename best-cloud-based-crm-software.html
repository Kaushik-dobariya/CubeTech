Customer Relationship Management

Managing employees, attendance, payroll, leaves, assets, and communication across an organization can be complex and time-consuming. Chatur HRMS is a comprehensive Human Resource Management System designed to simplify HR operations, improve employee experience, and provide organizations with complete visibility into their workforce.

From employee onboarding to payroll processing and workforce analytics, Chatur HRMS centralizes every HR function into one intelligent platform, helping businesses save time, reduce manual effort, improve compliance, and enhance productivity.

Why Chatur HRMS?

Centralized Workforce Management

Keep all employee information, records, documents, attendance, payroll, and assets in one secure platform.

Increased Operational Efficiency

Automate repetitive HR tasks such as attendance tracking, leave calculations, payroll processing, and document generation.

Better Employee Experience

Empower employees with self-service access to personal information, leave balances, payroll documents, and company updates.

Accurate Decision-Making

Gain real-time insights through dashboards and reports that help HR leaders make data-driven decisions.

Enhanced Compliance & Security

Maintain audit-ready records, secure document storage, and compliance-friendly payroll exports.
