Every solution is designed to improve productivity, reduce manual effort, and support long-term business growth.
  • 03. What is IT Staff Augmentation, and how can it benefit my business?
    IT Staff Augmentation is a flexible outsourcing model that allows businesses to extend their existing development team with experienced software professionals without the cost and commitment of hiring full-time employees. At Cubeon Technologies, we provide dedicated software developers, mobile app developers, web developers, QA engineers, UI/UX designers, DevOps engineers, and project managers who seamlessly integrate with your internal team. Whether you need additional expertise for a short-term project or long-term product development, our Staff Augmentation services help you accelerate delivery, reduce recruitment time, lower operational costs, and maintain complete control over your project.
  • 04. Why should I choose Cubeon Technologies for Staff Augmentation services?
    Cubeon Technologies focuses on providing highly skilled professionals who become a true extension of your organization. We carefully match developers based on your project requirements, technical stack, industry experience, and preferred working hours. Our transparent communication, flexible engagement models, and commitment to delivering quality software ensure that your business can scale development resources quickly without compromising productivity, security, or project timelines.
  • 05. What technologies do your software developers specialize in?
    Our development team has extensive experience building enterprise-grade software solutions using modern technologies including Python, Flask, Django, ASP.NET, C#, Java, PHP, React, Angular, Vue.js, JavaScript, TypeScript, Flutter, React Native, Kotlin, Swift, SQL Server, PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQLite, MongoDB, Docker, Kubernetes, Microsoft Azure, Amazon Web Services (AWS), and RESTful APIs. This diverse technology expertise enables us to develop scalable web applications, mobile applications, cloud-based systems, and custom enterprise software tailored to your business objectives.
  • 06. What is Data Scraping, and why do businesses need it?
    Data Scraping, also known as Web Scraping or Web Data Extraction, is the automated process of collecting publicly available information from websites and online platforms for business intelligence, market research, competitive analysis, lead generation, price monitoring, and data analytics. Businesses rely on accurate and up-to-date information to make strategic decisions, and manual data collection is often slow and inefficient. Cubeon Technologies develops secure, scalable, and automated data scraping solutions that extract structured information efficiently while respecting applicable laws and website usage policies.
  • 07. What kind of websites can Cubeon Technologies scrape?
    Our data scraping solutions support a wide variety of publicly accessible websites, including eCommerce platforms, online marketplaces, government portals, real estate websites, business directories, travel portals, educational websites, financial platforms, product catalogs, news websites, public databases, and job portals. Every project is carefully evaluated to ensure the most suitable extraction method while prioritizing reliability, data quality, and compliance with applicable legal and contractual requirements.
  • 08. Can you automate recurring Data Scraping projects?
    Yes. Cubeon Technologies develops intelligent automated data scraping systems that continuously collect updated information on predefined schedules such as hourly, daily, weekly, or monthly. The extracted data can be automatically cleaned, validated, transformed, and delivered to your preferred destination, including SQL databases, cloud storage, Excel files, CSV files, JSON APIs, Google Sheets, dashboards, or business intelligence platforms. This automation eliminates repetitive manual work and ensures your organization always has access to current and actionable information.
  • 09. What is Data Migration, and when is it required?
    Data Migration is the structured process of transferring business-critical information from one database, application, server, or cloud platform to another while preserving accuracy, consistency, and data integrity. Organizations typically require data migration when upgrading legacy software, moving to cloud infrastructure, replacing outdated business systems, consolidating multiple databases, or implementing new ERP, CRM, or enterprise applications. Cubeon Technologies carefully plans and executes every migration to minimize downtime, eliminate data loss, and ensure business continuity throughout the transition.
  • 10. How does Cubeon Technologies ensure secure and accurate Data Migration?
    Every Data Migration project begins with a comprehensive assessment of your existing systems, followed by detailed migration planning, data mapping, validation, testing, backup creation, and post-migration verification. Our team uses secure transfer methods, encryption protocols, integrity checks, and rollback strategies to safeguard sensitive business information. We support migrations between SQL Server, PostgreSQL, MySQL, SQLite, Oracle, MongoDB, Microsoft Access, cloud databases, and custom enterprise applications, ensuring that your data remains secure, complete, and fully operational after migration.
  • 11. Why should I invest in custom Mobile App Development instead of using off-the-shelf applications?
    Off-the-shelf mobile applications often include unnecessary features while lacking the functionality your business actually needs. Custom Mobile App Development enables your organization to build an application specifically designed around your workflows, customer experience, and long-term business goals. Cubeon Technologies develops secure, high-performance Android, iOS, and cross-platform mobile applications using Flutter, React Native, Kotlin, Swift, and modern backend technologies. Every application is optimized for speed, scalability, intuitive user experience, security, and future enhancements.
  • 12. What is Custom Web Application Development?
    Custom Web Application Development involves designing and developing browser-based software tailored specifically to your organization's operational requirements rather than relying on generic software solutions. Unlike standard applications, custom web applications streamline business processes, improve efficiency, automate repetitive tasks, integrate with existing systems, and provide greater flexibility as your business grows. Cubeon Technologies develops scalable, secure, and responsive web applications for startups, SMEs, and enterprises using modern frameworks and cloud technologies.
