Freequently Asked Question

  • 01. What is data scraping?
    Data scraping is the process of automatically extracting information from websites, online platforms, and digital sources into a structured format.
  • 02.What type of data can you extract?
    We can extract product details, business leads, pricing data, reviews, contact information, listings, analytics data, and more.
  • 03. Can you scrape dynamic or JavaScript websites?
    Yes. We can scrape static, dynamic, and JavaScript-rendered websites using advanced technologies.
  • 04. In which formats will I receive the data?
    We provide data in Excel, CSV, JSON, Google Sheets, SQL databases, APIs, or custom formats.
  • 05. Is the scraped data cleaned and organized?
    Yes. We clean, structure, validate, and organize all datasets before delivery.
  • 06. Can scraped data be integrated into my software or CRM?
    Yes. We can integrate extracted data directly into your applications, dashboards, APIs, or CRM systems.
  • 07. Do you provide custom scraping solutions?
    Yes. Every project is customized according to your business requirements and target websites.
